EAZESPOT - Effortless Working

A Unified Single interface for enhancing your workplace productivity.

Eazespot is a single window interface, which integrates communication and collaborative tools like email, task, chats, calendar, sales pipeline management and data drive, eliminating the need to work on multiple platforms.

Teams today are completely surrounded by multiple communication tools, which don’t speak to each other. Each one of them solves a different purpose, and hence cannot be eliminated from our lives. The use of tools like WhatsApp/Slack for individual and group Chats, Outlook/Gmail for professional Mails, Asana/Jira for Task Management, OneDrive/G-Drive for Data storage and sharing, Salesforce/Pipedrive for Lead Management, etc., are commonly used, creating a complex structure in our systems and our minds, and most of the times failing us when a critical data has to be searched. Information is available in silos, and analytics, tracking or linking of data is far from reality.

Eazespot provides the power of all these to you in an easy-to-use, comprehensive and reliable, single pre-integrated window - which enhances your business productivity, improves your time efficiency and keeps you updated on the go.
